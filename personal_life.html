Personal Life

Theodosia Raincloud Purr spent her first months learning to hunt on the streets. After becoming trapped in the wheelwell of a car, the humans across the street were so impressed by her perserverence through the difficult situation that they immediately hired her to teach them the proper social skills so many humans tragically never learn. It is difficult work, but she has made lifelong friends through the experience, and learned useful skills. However, the limited treats and trips outside have inspired her to begin broadening her horizons and seek a position that actually gives her adequate payment.
